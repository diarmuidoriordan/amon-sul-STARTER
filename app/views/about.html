About Us: Our History, and our Mission

"This was once the great watchtower of Amon Sûl..."

As the Third Age waned with the end of The War of the Ring, magic disappeared from Middle Earth, and with it any ability of the Free Peoples to accurately scry and predict the weather.

With the subsequent industrialisation of the lands, beginning in the Kingdom of Gondor, hope returned to the peoples of Middle Earth as study and innovation gave birth to new marvels of ingenuity and technology.

In time this led to the rebuilding of the great watchtower of Amon Sûl, and where before it would keep a vigilant guard against oncoming armies, in its place WeatherTop Inc. has built a modern and advanced weather prediction service, putting the power of the Palantíri into the hands of the people.

About Us: The WeatherTop 1000 System

"And then her heart changed, or at least she understood it;
and the winter passed, and the sun shone upon her."

Developed in partnership with the Institute of Science in Minas Tirith, and the Ministry of the Environment in Osgiliath, the WeatherTop 1000 system is a solar-charged and battery-powered weather readings station designed for the Fourth Age.

On this website you can view the readings from your WeatherTop 1000 stations (as many as are linked to your account).

Future updates will enable additional features, so keep checking back for more!
